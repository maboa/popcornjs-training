Why Popcorn?

Popcorn allows you to attach actions to time points in audio and video. It helps make media more web-like.

Who's it for?

Anyone that wants to create a rich interactive application based on media. You can choose from or add to a wide selection of plugins to augment data to your media from sources all over the web.

It's great for telling stories!
