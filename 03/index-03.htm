The Future is Mobile

Brace yourselves ...

  • Stock Android browser may or may not support video, there are versions (2.2) with video support but no audio. Wat?
  • Browsers on mobile generally delegate codec support to the underlying OS
  • iOS on iPhones/iPod will pop video into the Quicktime player. On iPads it will not.
  • iOS and possibly Android will not allow you to play media simultaneously
  • Mobile browsers generally ignore autoplay (requiring user initiated events to start playing)
  • Volume changes are usually ignored. The user is in charge damnit!
  • Opera Mini and other proxy browsers, simply will not work.
  • Flash is rarely supported on mobile. (No fallbacks)
  • There are mobile browsers that we have never heard of. (Though most of them are based on WebKit).
