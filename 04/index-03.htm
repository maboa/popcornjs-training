Transcripts

Transcribing audio gives you many advantages:

  • Accessibility
  • Searchability
  • Navigability (is this a word?)

Transcribing audio to word-level timings gives you extra! ie. Accurate playbcak points.

How do we transcribe audio/video accurately?

Pure speech to text currently requires human input and so usually needs to be paid for.

Options :

  • Third-party services (3playmedia.com, Koemei.com)
  • Do It Yourself
  • Convert from less accurate formats (and tweak).
